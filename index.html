What We’re All About

The A List Supper Club is a cozy, invite-only dining experience hosted by Amanda and Amruta. We bring people together around a beautiful table and a thoughtfully planned menu.

Each dinner has a theme, a curated 4-course meal, a signature drink, and a relaxed vibe so new connections feel natural, not forced.

Come for the food, stay for the people — and leave with a few new friends.

What’s Cooking Next

We host small, themed suppers in and around North Texas. Spots are limited so RSVPs are always required.
